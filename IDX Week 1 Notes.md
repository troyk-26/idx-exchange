**Week 1**

Key feature descriptions

* **OriginalListPrice** \- initial price of property  
* **ClosePrice** \- amount paid by buyer to seller for property  
* **PropertyType** \- Residential, Lease, Income, Land, Mobile, Commercial Sale, etc…  
* **LivingArea** \- total liveable space  
* **ListPrice** \- current price of property  
* **DaysOnMarket** \- number of days on market (according to Multiple Listing Service rules)  
* **ParkingTotal** \- number of parking spaces included in sale  
* **PropertySubType** \- e.g. Single Family Residence, Condominium, Manufactured on Land, Townhouse, Multi Family, Office, Retail, etc.  
* **LotSizeAcres** \- total acres of property (related to LotSizeArea and LotSizeUnits)  
* **YearBuilt** \- when occupancy permit was granted for the property  
* **BathroomsTotalInteger** \- number of bathrooms (not accounting for half or full status)  
* **BuildingAreaTotal** \- total area of finished and unfinished space  
* **BedroomsTotal** \- total number of bedrooms  
* **Stories** \- number of floors for the property being sold  
* **Levels** \- similar to Stories but includes areas separated partial sets of staircases  
* **LotSizeArea** \- total area of property  
* **MainLevelBedrooms** \- number of bedrooms on main or entry level  
* **GarageSpaces** \- number of parking spaces in garage  
* **LotSizeSquareFeet** \- total square footage of property
